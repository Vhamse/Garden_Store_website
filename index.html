Key Features

1. Plant Selection:The application would offer a user-friendly interface allowing individuals to select plants suitable for their local climate and available space.
2. Gardening Tips: Providing real-time, location-specific gardening tips, including planting seasons, soil types, and pest control advice.
3. Community Engagement: Creating a gardening community where users can share their experiences, seek advice, and even trade or sell excess produce.
4. E-commerce Integration: Facilitating the purchase of gardening equipment, seeds, and soil through partnerships with local and online vendors.
5. Health Tracking: Users can monitor the health of their plants through photos, and the app would offer suggestions and solutions in case of issues.
